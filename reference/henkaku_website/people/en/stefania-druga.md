Stefania Druga is a research scientist at Sakana.ai, formerly at Google DeepMind, passionate about the intersection of AI, science, and education. Through her research, she develops cutting-edge tools and platforms that empower both humans and machines to engage in real-time co-discovery and multimodal learning. Her projects include the Real-Time AI Co-Scientist platform and multimodal AI applications for kids, such as the Scratch AI Copilot. Stefania is also a dedicated educator, teaching diverse audiences how to build meaningful AI-powered tools. Based in Tokyo and collaborating globally, Stefania envisions a future where inquiry drives innovation, and where asking salient questions transforms learning and discovery across generations.
